Scope and Limitations

This study utilized worn-out soft brooms in the production of particle board. It was conducted at the Mataas Na Paaralang Juan C. Laya. The study will start on June 24, 2017. The tests conducted were nailing endurance and breaking strength at E.B Laboratory, Urdaneta City.
