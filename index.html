Background of the Study

Modern packaging and disposable items become the major cause of land pollution around the world. Tons of litters are thrown along streets and highways, in parks, and along beaches. Some wastes such as worn-out soft brooms are biodegradable, decompose relatively quickly, and return to the soil. Same scenario is observed in the Philippines. Tons of solid wastes are collected in the cities, towns and barangays every day.

In Mataas Na Paaralang Juan C. Laya, the surrounding is very clean and green. But there were lots of wastes that were thrown in the garbage which includes worn-out soft brooms.

So the researchers conducted this study if worn-out soft broomscan be used in the production of particle board to lessen the causes of solid waste.








Statement of the Problem

General Problem:
The possibility to produce particle board from worn-out soft brooms

Specific Problem:
= >What is the best combination of binder for the waste component?
=> What is the nail endurance and breaking strength of the particle board from worn-out soft broom?
=> Is the particle board from worn-out soft brooms comparable to the commercial particle board as to nailing endurance and breaking strength?
